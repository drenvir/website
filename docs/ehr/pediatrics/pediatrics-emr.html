Pediatrics EHR

Overview

ChARM EHR for Pedicatricians supports you as you deal with the treatment of medical, developmental and behavioral conditions in children, apart from tracking wellness and immunization. ChARM EHR is ONC-ATCB certified for meaningful use for ambulatory setup. Being web based, it allows you to access your patients' records anytime, anywhere with the help of mobile devices with internet access.

  • Vaccination Tracking

    ChARM EHR allows you to track vaccination/immunizations administered to your patients. For every vaccination, you can track the CVX code, manufacturer, Lot No., dosage, date of administration, etc. With ChARM EHR you can also schedule appointments for the next vaccination and patients can receive notifications about their next visit for vaccinations.

  • Immunization Registry

    According to government regulations, it may be necessary to notify the immunization registry with the list of immunizations administered for children. ChARM EHR allows you to export the immunization history of each patient as standard HL7 mesages, which can be uploaded to the immunization registry.

  • Growth Chart

    Growth Chart allows you to track the growth of infants and chlidren. With ChARM EHR, you can track height, weight, head circumference, BMI, etc. of each child against standard percentiles.

  • Pediatric EMR Templates

    ChARM EHR provides customizable templates to help you treat wide variety of conditions in children.

  • Track Developmental Milestones

    ChARM EHR allows you to track developmental milestones, the functional skills that the child needs to exhibit at certain age range. It includes gross motor, fine motor, language, cognitive, social skills, speech development, etc.

  • Manage Behavioral Conditions

    ChARM EHR helps you to manage and track behavioral conditions such as ADHD, Autism, Spasms, Bruxism, etc., where early recognition and intervention are crucial for best outcomes.

  • Online Appointment Scheduling

    ChARM EHR allows you to publish your schedules through the patient portal as well as your practice website. This enables patients to book appointments on their own, thus improving front office productivity.

  • Patient Portal

    Patients can request appointments online and interact with the care team by sending messages securely. It also allows them to track health vitals, appointments and healthcare events.
