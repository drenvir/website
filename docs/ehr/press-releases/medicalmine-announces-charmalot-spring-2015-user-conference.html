MedicalMine Announces ChARMALOT Spring 2015 User Conference

PLEASANTON, Calif., Apr 13, 2015 (BUSINESS WIRE) -- MedicalMine today announced ChARMALOT Spring 2015, its third user conference for independent medical practitioners, practice managers, and software developers. This year`s conference covers both introductory and in-depth product training on popular features and capabilities and announces recent integrations with partner systems such as Twilio, Sfax, and Bluefin Payment Systems. Mobile companion apps will be demonstrated and select partners will showcase their solutions. A special track for developers and partners will focus on the capabilities of ChARM EHR APIs.

ChARMALOT Spring 2015 will be held on April 25-26, 2015, at the Zoho Corp. headquarters and event center in Pleasanton, Calif. Attendees may register at https://charmtracker.com/charmalot/index.html. Limited, early bird registration for the first 50 registrants is $99. The regular registration price is $149.

“Last year’s conference proved extremely valuable for independent physicians and practitioners,” said Pramila Srinivasan, CEO, MedicalMine. “Our attendees told us that they learned a lot from our product training and also from simply networking, especially with other practitioners advancing their practices through innovative technology. We are aiming higher this year. We have added expert speakers in billing, insurance and adjacent areas. We have designed a number of interactive panel sessions, live feedback and discussion groups for specialties to facilitate conversations, enabling attendees to connect, share and learn.”

The training tracks will cover product features for ChARM Practice Management, ChARM EHR and PHR. Demonstrations will include partner integrations for e-Prescription, Laboratories, SMS, Messaging, Payment Processing, e-Faxing and others. Presentations on ChARM RCM and billing services will include best practices and insurance billing workflows that attendees will use to improve reimbursement.

The developer track will discuss APIs and mobile applications that are part of the ChARM ecosystem of connected devices.

“We look forward to learning from attendees and experts, so that we can continue to drive innovation in our products,” said Srinivasan.

For more information on ChARM EHR, visit www.charmehr.com. To get breaking ChARM EHR news, follow the company on Twitter at @charmtracker and on Facebook at www.facebook.com/charmtracker

About MedicalMine Inc.

MedicalMine Inc. is a Pleasanton, California software company that brings cloud-based technology to medicine to increase patient participation and enhance patient outcomes, while also improving productivity at medical practices. Its first product, ChARMtracker – a patient portal – has gained thousands of users. The second, ChARM EHR – focused on the practice – is popular with small and medium sized medical practices worldwide. Visit us at http://www.charmehr.com
