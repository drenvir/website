Integrated Patient Payment Service

Greetings!

ChARM EHR is pleased to announce that you can now process patient payments directly within the ChARM EHR software using Bluefin Payment Systems! Both ChARM EHR and Bluefin recognize the necessity of providing our clients with a robust payment platform protected by state-of-the-art security features including point-to-point data encryption (P2PE).

Please email info@charmehr.com to get signed up today.

With our integrated patient payment solution, ChARM EHR clients can:

  • Collect patient payments at the ‘Point of Care.’ Practices can collect the patient payment information while checking in the patient through ChARM EHR.
  • Eliminate posting errors with automated payment entry.
  • Reduce collection efforts and improve payment workflow.
  • Create recurring payment plans using the Bluefin Card on File feature.

And every ChARM EHR client enjoys:

  • All Payment Products: Process credit/debit cards, ACH transactions, and recurring billing in Ecommerce, mobile and POS solutions.
  • Highest Level of Security: Bluefin is a PCI-DSS compliant provider, and our products, including tokenization and P2PE, help secure our clients’ transactions.
  • One-on-one Customer Support: Your questions are answered quickly and directly by one of our in-house team members.

Bluefin leads payment processing with the most robust and secure variety of payments for merchants of all sizes. And as a participating organization of the PCI Standards Security Council, you can rest assured that every product you use from Bluefin has been built with the highest level of security in mind. Please email info@charmehr.com to get signed up today.

All the best,
Team ChARM EHR
