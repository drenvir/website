How do I receive labcorp results into ChARM EHR?
Inform your LabCorp representative about your decision to go with ChARM EHR, and ask them to contact us. LabCorp will initiate the process to interface your lab results to ChARM EHR.

How to group LabCorp results?
From Labs -> Lab Results, we can view the LabCorp results grouped together. You can even take a print of the grouped results.

How to re-assign the reviewer of Lab results?
In Labs -> Lab Results,

  • Select a particular lab result
  • Change the reviewer
  • Click on “Save”.

How to add new labs, tests and parameters?
In Labs -> Customize Labs, new labs, tests and parameters can be added and customized.
