What are the requisites for using ChARM EHR?
ChARM EHR is an online application. There is no hardware or software installation involved. All you need is a web browser to connect to the application and use.

Is my data safe?

ChARM uses 256-bit encryption and follows stringent software security measures as per HIPAA regulations to keep your data safe. Your data is backed up regularly and replicated across secure location for any emergency.

If I have a question or a feature request how do I contact you?
You can always send us your queries to support@charmehr.com . We will get back to you as quickly as possible

Does ChARM EHR support HL7 based transactions?
ChARM EHR supports HL7 based transactions. Currently, HL7 based lab results (ORU) are being received electronically from labs.

What is meaningful use, and how does it apply to Electronic Health Record (EHR) Incentive Programs?
In 2009, the US congress passed an act for economic stimulus package (ARRA), under which physicians and providers can qualify for incentives, if they demonstrate “Meaningful Use” of EHR. Under this program, providers can earn up to $39000 in Medicare and $63750 in Medicaid incentives respectively, over a period of six years. ChARM EHR is fully-certified for meaningful by Drummond Group, which means providers using ChARM EHR in their practice can apply for the incentive program.

Which drug database is being used by ChARM EHR?
LexiComp database is used for drug reference and interaction process. Drug-Drug, Drug-Allergy & Drug-Food interactions are captured under the interaction process.

Are CPT codes bundled in ChARM EHR?
No, CPT codes are not bundled by default. Please configure CPT codes required for your practice from “Settings -> Treatment/Consultation Codes” section.

How do I get started with connecting to labs?
We do support connecting to your labs through HL7 interface. Please send us a mail to support@charmehr.com with the lab of your choice, so that we can help you.

How do I add my first patient?
To add a new patient,

  • Go to "Patients" tab
  • Click on "New Patient"
  • Provide the details, viz., "First Name", "Last Name". "Date of Birth" etc. If you want to provide access to the patient portal, select "Send PHR invitation mail to patient" and provide the patient's email address in the "Email" field
  • Click on "Add Patient"

How can I delete a patient record from my EHR?
Patient record once created, cannot be deleted. If needed, the practice can de-activate the patient. To de-activate the patient, change the status of the patient from “Active” to “Deactivate” in the patient dashboard.

I have patients’ past visit details in paper. Can I import them into ChARM?
You can scan the old paper charts and upload them either under “Patient Details -> Past Encounters” section or “Patient Details -> Documents” section.

How to store old patient lab records in ChARM?
In the Lab section, you can use the “Import Results” to import patient’s old records.

How can I review the recent access and activities done in my EHR?
You can view all the practice activities through “Settings -> Audit Trails” section. Only practice admin can view the access and activities performed by all practice members.
