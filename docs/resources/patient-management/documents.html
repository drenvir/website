Documents

  • Import Documents
  • Scan Documents
  • View Documents

Import Documents

  1. Select 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Select the 'Documents' tab.
  3. Select 'Import from Local Drive' or 'Import from ChARM Documents' option.
  4. Select the documents to be imported.
  5. Choose the 'Document Type' and 'Date'. Click on 'Upload' to upload the patient document.

Scan Documents

Scanner interface plugin in ChARM helps you to scan patient's documents directly into the system. ChARM can connect to any TWAIN compliant scanners. Most scanners available in the market are TWAIN compliant by default. You can check your scanner specification to find out whether it is TWAIN compatible or contact your vendor to know that.

  1. Select 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Select the 'Documents' tab.
  3. Click on 'Import > Scan Document' option.
  4. On your first usage, you will be asked to install a scanner plugin based on your operating system and browser used. Install the plugin and proceed to scan the document.

View Documents

  1. Select 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Select the 'Documents' tab
  3. Click on the gear icon against the document you want to view and select 'Open in New Tab' option
  4. The document will be get opened.
  5. You can also share or download the document. The documents can also be moved between various folders.
