Patient Demographics

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab, and click on the patient whose demographic details you want to view/edit.
  2. Click on 'Patient Details' and select 'Edit demographics' option.
  3. Make the required changes and click on 'Save'

Medical History

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab and select the patient.
  2. Click on 'Patient Details' tab
  3. Select 'Medical history' tab
  4. Select 'View Active' option from the drop box next to 'Diagnoses History', to view the active list of diagnosis.
  5. Select 'View History' to view the past medical history of diagnosis.

Health Vitals

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab
  2. Click on the Patient profile whose health vitals you want to enter
  3. Click on 'Patient Details'
  4. Further, click on 'Health Vitals' tab and then select 'Add vitals' link.
  5. Choose the date of entry from the 'Date' field and enter the health vitals of the patient, viz., Height, Weight, Blood Pressure, etc. and click on 'Save'

Plot Health Vitals

  1. To plot the health vitals, Click on the 'Plot' option.
  2. Select the parameter you want to plot from the drop box. The graph for the parameter will be plotted.
  3. You can also import the past health vitals of the patient using the 'Import Vitals' option.
  4. To do this, create an Excel sheet with the following parameters as comma separated values. The order should be Date[dd/mm/yyyy], Weight (kgs), Height (cms), Head Circuference (cms), BP Systolic, BP Diastolic, Pulse.
  5. Browse to the Excel sheet and click on 'Import' to import the past health vitals.

Medications

  1. Go to 'Patients' and select the patient
  2. Click on 'Patient Details'
  3. Click on 'Medications' tab and the click on 'Add Medication' link.
  4. Search for the drug name from the master list and click on 'Add' to add the medication.
  5. If you could not find the drug in the master list, you can add it as a 'Custom Drug'
  6. The medication will be added.
  7. Similarly, prescription can be added from the 'Add Prescription' option.
  8. Enter the details of the precription.
  9. There are two ways in which you can add the dosage, 'Default Dosage' and 'Custom Dosage'. Choose the dosage option according to your need.
  10. Click on 'Add' to add the presciption

Allergies

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Select 'Patient Details'
  3. Click on 'Allergies' tab and the click on 'Add allergies' link.
  4. If the patient is allergic to any drug/food/environment, it can be addded here.
  5. Add the allergens to the 'Allergen' field and choose the 'Type' of allergy. You can also add more details like when the allergy was observed, severity of the allergy, etc.
  6. Click 'OK' to add the allergy for the patient.

Questionnaires

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Select 'Patient Details'
  3. Click on 'Questionnaires' tab.
  4. You can view all the filled up questionnaire by the patient.

Insurance Details

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab and select the patient whose insurance details has to be filled in.
  2. Click on 'Patient Details' and select 'Insurance' tab
  3. Click on 'Add Insurance' option.
  4. Key-in the insurance details of the selected patient.
  5. The insurance details will get added.

Pharmacy

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Click on 'Patient Details' and select 'Pharmacy' tab.
  3. Click on 'Add e-Rx Pharmacy' option to add the pharmacy
  4. Search for the pharmacy name and add it to the list of e-Rx pharmacies.
  5. You can also add a custom pharmacy using the 'Add Custom Pharmacy' option or 'Select from Pharmacy Directory'
  6. The custom pharmacies will get added under the head 'Other Pharmacy'

Patient Facesheet

Practitioner can get a quick overview about the patient by viewing the patient's 'Face Sheet'

  1. Go to Patient Dashboard and click on 'Fact Sheet' to view their summary.
  2. You can customize the fact sheet, by clicking on the gear icon.
  3. Select the sections you want in the Face Sheet, by selecting the check boxes.
  4. You can also print the fact sheet by clicking on the download icon.

Timeline View

Timeline view shows the patient's medical records in a pictorial fashion. Records included in the timeline view are Appointments, Intake Forms, Encounters, Diagnoses, Prescription, Lab Orders, Image Orders, Referrals, Lab Results, Image Results and Messages.

  1. Go to Patient Dashboard. Click on the 'Timeline' tab.
  2. The timeline will appear as shown below. Records are shown in the chronological order with recent entry on the top.
  3. You can also filter a particular section of the timeline you want to view.

TagCloud View

Tagcloud view provides a bird's eye view of the patient's medical data. It is a color coded visual representation of Diagnoses, Prescriptions, Supplements, and Abnormal Lab Record parameters. Different patient data points are depicted in different font sizes based on calculated weights using custom algorithms.

  1. Go to patient dashboard and click on 'Tag Cloud' option to view the tag cloud.
  2. Tag Cloud can be regenerated by choosing the various parameter types like Rx, Diagnoses, Supplements, Allergies, Lab, etc.
