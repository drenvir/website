Upload Lab Records

  1. Select 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Select the 'Lab Records' tab
  3. You can drag and drop the PDF files or enter results manually.
  4. On clicking 'Enter Results Manually' option, a popup window will open, where you can select the tests from the drop box and enter the results against each lab test.

Scan Lab Records

Scanner interface plugin in ChARM helps you to scan patient's past lab records directly into the system. ChARM can connect to any TWAIN compliant scanners. Most scanners available in the market are TWAIN compliant by default. You can check your scanner specification to find out whether it is TWAIN compatible or contact your vendor to know that.

  1. Select 'Patients' tab and select the patient
  2. Select the 'Lab Records' tab.
  3. Click on 'Scan Lab Records' option. Select the 'Upload Type' as 'Lab Record', select the lab name, tests, etc. and click on 'Upload'
  4. Note: On your first usage, you will be asked to install a scanner plugin based on your operating system and browser used. Install the plugin and proceed to scan the document.

View Lab Records

To view the lab records of the patient

  1. Select 'Patients' tab and select the patient.
  2. Click on 'Lab Records' tab.
  3. Click on the tests to view the results.

Plot Lab Results

  1. Select 'Patients' tab and select the patient.
  2. Click on 'Lab Records' tab.
  3. Click on the lab test to view the results.
  4. Against the lab test, click on the 'Plot' icon to plot the lab results over a period of time.
  5. A popup will appear with the plotted result
