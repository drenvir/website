Imaging Results

  • Review Image Results
  • Print Image Results
  • Delete Image Result

Review Image Results

  1. Go to 'Images > Image Results' tab.
  2. Click on the gear icon against the imaging result of the patient and select the 'Review' option.
  3. Review the imaging results and add your comments. Once completed, click on 'Review' to sign the imaging result.

Print Image Results

  1. Go to 'Images > Image Results' tab.
  2. Click on the gear icon against the image result to be printed and select the 'Export' option. Save the exported document to print it.

Delete Image Result

To delete the image results

  1. Go to 'Images > Image Results' tab.
  2. Click on the gear icon against the patient's test result and click on 'Delete'.
  3. Confirm deletion by selecting 'OK' in the confirmation popup message.
