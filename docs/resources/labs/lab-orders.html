Ordering labs during Consultation

When you want to place a lab diagnostic test during consultation of the patient, follow the steps given below:

  1. Go to 'Patient' tab
  2. Select the patient you want to refer for lab test from the patient's dashboard
  3. Click on 'Open New Consultation'
  4. Scroll down to 'Recommendations' section and select the 'Lab' tab. You can choose the lab tests, either from 'Instant Add' or 'Choose from Templates' option.
  5. Choose a lab name
  6. From the list of tests, select the test(s) you want to order for the patient.
  7. Click on 'order' and the selected test will get added.
  8. Click on 'Save' button to save the changes.

Add Lab Order without Consultation

  1. Go to 'Labs' tab and select 'Lab Orders' section.
  2. Click on 'Add Lab Order' button.

  3. You can add a new lab order either by using the 'Instant Add' or 'Choose from Templates' option.
  4. Follow the steps as in 'Lab order during consultation' to place the lab order.
  5. Once your lab orders are placed, you can print the lab order by clicking the 'Print' option.

Print Specimen Label

To print the specimen label, follow the steps given below:

  1. Click on the gear icon against the lab order, for which you wish to print the specimen label.
  2. A popup menu will appear asking for the number of labels to be printed.
  3. Enter the number of labels to be printed and click on 'Print'

Key-In Test Results

  1. Select the 'Labs' tab
  2. click on 'Lab orders'
  3. select the patient whose lab test results are to be updated
  4. Click on the 'gear button' and select the option 'Import Results'
  5. Key in the lab results against each test performed.
  6. Add comments if any and click on 'Save'
  7. The updated results will be shown with a green tick mark.

Mark Order as Completed

When the lab test has been completed then the test can be marked as complete.

  1. Go to 'Labs' > 'Lab Orders'
  2. Click on the gear icon against the lab order and select 'Mark as Completed' option
  3. Click 'Yes' on the confirmation dialog.

Print Lab Order

  1. Go to 'Labs' tab and click on 'Lab Orders' section
  2. Click on the gear icon against the order and click on 'Export as PDF' option to print the lab order.
