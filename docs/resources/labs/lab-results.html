Lab Results

  • Review Lab Results
  • Print Lab Results
  • Plot Lab Results
  • Delete Lab Result

Review Lab Results

  1. Go to 'Labs > Lab Results' tab
  2. Click on the gear icon against the test result of the patient and select the 'Review' option
  3. Review the lab results and add your comments. Once completed, click on 'Review' to sign the lab result.

Print Lab Results

  1. Go to 'Labs > Lab Results' tab.
  2. Click on the gear icon against the lab result to be printed and select the 'Export as PDF' option. Save the exported document to print it.

Plot Lab Results

  1. Go to 'Labs < Lab Results' tab
  2. Click on the gear icon against the lab result to be plotted for a given patient.
  3. Select 'Plot' option, a window will appear asking to opt for the test you want to plot. Select the test(s) by selecting the checkboxes =
  4. The test you want will be plotted

Delete Lab Result

  1. Go to 'Labs > Lab Results' tab
  2. Click on the gear icon against the patient's test result and click on 'Delete'.
  3. Confirm deletion by selecting 'OK' in the confirmation popup message.
