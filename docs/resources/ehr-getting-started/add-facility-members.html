Add Facility Members

Practice members can be added to ChARM EHR by following the steps given below.

  1. Go to Settings > Facility > Facility Members.
  2. Click on "New Member" link
  3. Enter the mandatory fields, viz., First Name, Last Name, Login Id, Member Role, and Email Address of the member.   Note that the "Login Id" once configured for a member cannot be changed.  Please choose it wisely
  4. If multiple facilities are configured, then select the facilities to which the new member has to be associated.
  5. If you want to provide emergency privilege access, check the "Enable Emergency Access" option
  6. Click on "Add Member"
  7. Once the member is added, an email will be sent to the member's email address with their login credentials.
