Update Facility Details

Facility details & facility logo can be updated by following the steps given below:

  1. Go to Settings > Facility > Facility List
  2. Click on the "Edit" icon, against the facility you want to edit
  3. Upload your facility logo by clicking on "Upload Facillity Logo" link
  4. Update your facility details
  5. Click "Save" to save your changes
