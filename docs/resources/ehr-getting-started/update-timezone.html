Update TimeZone and Working Hours

You can configure your practice timezone and practice working hours by following the steps given below.  This is necessary to make sure your appointment scheduling screen shows the appropriate working hours for your practice.

  1. Go to Settings > Calendar > Facility Timings
  2. Choose the facility for which the timezone and working hours has to configured
  3. Choose your timezone in the "Facility Timezone" section ,and click on "Save"
  4. Configure your practice timings and holidays in the "Facility Working Hours" section, and click on "Save"
  5. Repeat the above process for each of your facility, if you have multiple facilities configured.
