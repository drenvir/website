Add Vaccine Order

To add an vaccine order to a patient, follow the steps given below:

  1. Go to 'Patients' tab
  2. Select the patient to go to the patient dashboard
  3. Open a new consultation for the patient
  4. In the 'Recommendations' section, select the 'Vaccines/Injections' tab. Click on 'Add > Vaccine' option. The following popup will be shown.
  5. Add the name of the vaccine. You can also add notes to the nurse or any specific comments to the vaccine order. Click on 'Add' to add the vaccine order. You can also add the vaccine from the pre-defined vaccine template

View Vaccine Orders

To view the vaccines ordered for the patient, follow the steps given below

  1. Go to 'Rx/Injections' tab, and select 'Vaccine Orders'.
  2. Click on the gear-icon against the patient's vaccine order and select the 'View' option to view/print the vaccine order
  3. Once the vaccine is administered, mark 'Administered' as 'Yes'. Also, key in the other details about the vaccine, viz., Administered Date, Administered Time, Route, Site, Manufacturer, Lot Number, etc. for future reference. Select 'Save'. The vaccine order will be shown under the status 'Administered'.
