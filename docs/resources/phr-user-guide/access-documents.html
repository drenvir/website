Access Documents

You can access the handouts, consent forms and other documents shared by your practice under ‘Documents’ section.

A notification icon is shown on top right corner of ‘Documents’ section, whenever you get new documents from your practice.

You will also receive an email notifying this.

To access the shared documents,

  1. Go to Documents section and select ‘Shared to me’ folder.
  2. Click on the file to preview it.

You can also download the files by using ‘Action’ > ‘Download’ menu.
