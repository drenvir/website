Menu 5 : Patient-Specific Education Resources

Measure Overview : Provide educational resources for patients.

Denominator : Number of unique patients seen by the Provider during the EHR reporting period.

Numerator : Number of patients in the denominator who are provided patient-specific education resources.

Stage 1 Goal : 10%

Exclusion : None

ChARM EHR Workflow :

If patient has PHR account, you can share handouts and educational materials from Encounter > Handouts section.

If patient doesn't have PHR account, you can provide paper handouts and manually check the 'Provide patient-specific education' option under Encounter > 'Meaningful Use Measures' section.

For more information, refer to the CMS guideline for this measure
