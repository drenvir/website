Menu 2: Electronic Notes

Measure Overview : Create electronic notes for patient encounter.

Denominator : Number of unique patients with at least one office visit during the EHR reporting period for Provider during the EHR reporting period.

Numerator : The number of unique patients in the denominator who have at least one electronic progress note from an eligible professional recorded as text searchable data

Stage 2 Goal : 30%

Exclusion : Provider who has no office visits during the EHR reporting period.

ChARM EHR Workflow : Follow the steps below to create electronic notes for patient encounter

  1. Open new encounter from Patient Dashboard or from Calendar > List view
  2. Choose the required Chart Type
  3. Enter SOAP notes
  4. You can key-in the notes or use predefined templates
  5. Save and sign the notes

For more information, refer to the CMS guideline for this measure
