Core 12 : Preventative Care

Measure Overview : Send reminder to patients who had two or more office visits within the 24 months before the beginning of the EHR reporting period, per patient preference when available.

Denominator : Number of unique patients who have had two or more office visits with the Provider in the 24 months prior to the beginning of the EHR reporting period.

Numerator : Number of patients in the denominator who were sent a reminder per patient preference when available during the EHR reporting period.

Stage 2 Goal : 10%

Exclusion : Provider who has had no office visits in the 24 months before the EHR reporting period

ChARM EHR Workflow :

Use the Patient Statistics or Patient Followup report under Analytics section to generate patient list for preventive or follow-up care.

Export the patient list and send reminders to patients based on their communication preference.

Once reminders are sent, use the 'MU Entry for Patient Reminder' link to update the patient list for MU calculation.

For more information, refer to the CMS guideline for this measure
