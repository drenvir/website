Core 14 : Medication Reconciliation

Measure Overview : Perform medication reconciliation when patients are referred to your practice by another practice.

Denominator : Number of transitions of care during the EHR reporting period for which the provider was the receiving party of the transition.

Numerator : Number of transitions of care in the denominator where medication reconciliation was performed.

Stage 2 Goal : 50%

Exclusion : Provider who don't receive any transitions of care during the EHR reporting period.

ChARM EHR Workflow : For patients that are referred to your practice, record the details below during encounter.

  1. Go to Encounter > 'Meaningful Use Measures' section
  2. Choose 'Transition of care incoming'
  3. Check if Summary of care record is received from patient.
  4. If summary of care record is received, specify whether you have reconciled the mediations from the summary of care record with patient's existing medication list in your practice.

For more information, refer to the CMS guideline for this measure
