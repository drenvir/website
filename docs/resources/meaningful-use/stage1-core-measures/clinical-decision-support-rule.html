Core 10 : Clinical Decision Support Rule

Measure Overview : Implement at least one clinical decision support (CDS) rule and have it enabled for the entire EHR reporting period.

Exclusion : None

ChARM EHR Workflow :

CDS rules implemented in ChARM EHR are listed under Settings > Encounter > CDS section.

Contact your Practice Administrator and enable at least one CDS rule for your role.

For more information, refer to the CMS guideline for this measure
