Web Embed Calendar

Calendar Web Embed feature enables you to embed your provider’s calendar in practice website and allows patients to view provider’s availability and book appointment themselves

Follow the steps below to embed your calendar in practice website

  • Update your Practice Name and Contact Details
  • Update Practice Timezone
  • Upload Your Photo
  • Update Degree and Specialization
  • Tell about yourself
  • Configure your Working Hours
  • Enable Online Appointments
  • create-web-embed

Upload Your Photo

Upload your photo under Settings > Account > ‘My Profile’ section

Update Degree and Specialization

Update your professional degree and medical specialization

Tell about yourself

  1. Tell a few words about yourself for patients to know about you more

Create Web Embed

  1. Administrator of your practice can create an embed code and include it in your practice website
  2. You can create an embed code for a single provider or for multiple providers
  3. Enter the name of hosting website
  4. Copy the generated code and paste it in your website content

Embedded View

Patients have to follow the below steps, to book appointment from your website

  1. Choose the provider
  2. Choose the required time slot
  3. Enter patient details
  4. Confirm the appointment




If embed code is created for a single provider, patients have to follow three steps, excluding the first step (provider selection) in the above sequence
