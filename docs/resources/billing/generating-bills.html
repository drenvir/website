Generating Bills

Providers can track all the encounters whether billed or not by choosing options from the drop down box on left corner "Encounters for Billing" or "Encounters-No billing required"

  1. Navigate to Billing
  2. Click on the left drop down box to view wether billing is required or not

Moving encounter from encounters for billing to billing not required

  1. Navigate to Billing
  2. Select the encounters
  3. click on the button "Billing not required"

Reverting from Encounters-Billing not required to Billing requuired

  1. Navigate to Billing
  2. Click on the drop down box and select the option Encounters - No Billing Required
  3. Click on the gear icon and select"Billing required"
